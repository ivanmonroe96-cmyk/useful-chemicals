Silver Liquid Mercury

High-purity silver liquid mercury certified at 99.99% purity. Suitable for precision tasks from scientific lab work to industrial applications.

Why Choose High-Purity Silver Liquid Mercury?

Purity matters when it comes to mercury, especially for sensitive applications. Our silver liquid mercury is certified with 99.99% purity (free from heavy metals), ensuring it's suitable for precision tasks, from scientific lab work to industrial applications.

Common Uses
  • Mining and Extraction: Widely used in gold extraction due to its binding properties.
  • Medical Applications: Useful in dental amalgams and some medical devices.
  • Manufacturing: Employed in various industrial tools and measurement devices.
  • Scientific Research: Essential for laboratories requiring controlled, high-purity materials.
Product Specifications
  • Purity Level 99.99% certified
  • Packaging Safe, tamper-resistant cylinders (25g–5kg)
  • Safety Heavy metal-free for precision tasks
  • Delivery Available to all permissible states within the USA
Handling and Safety Precautions

Silver mercury is a powerful substance but requires careful handling. Always store it in a temperature-controlled environment, ideally in containers designed to prevent leakage and minimize evaporation.

  • Keep in well-ventilated areas.
  • Wear protective gloves and eye protection.
  • Avoid prolonged exposure to vapors by handling in controlled, low-temperature environments.
